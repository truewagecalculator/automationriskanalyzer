Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Automation Risk Analyzer (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use this site.

Cookies, analytics, and advertising

We may use cookies or similar technologies for site functionality, measurement, and advertising. If advertising is enabled (e.g., Google AdSense), third-party partners may set cookies to serve and measure ads.

  • Third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this or other websites.

Note: Ad and cookie behavior depends on your region and the configuration of advertising partners.

How we use information

  • To provide tool results (risk score, tasks, skills, timeline).
  • To maintain site security and prevent abuse.
  • To understand site usage trends and improve the experience.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the site (hosting, analytics, advertising, security) as needed to provide the service.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for operational purposes, security, and performance. Aggregated statistics may be kept longer.

Your choices

  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings (some functionality may be affected).
  • If advertising is enabled, you may be able to manage ad personalization via the ad provider’s settings.

Children’s privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy
